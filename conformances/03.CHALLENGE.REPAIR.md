# AAP Conformance Challenge And Repair

This artifact defines a canonical bounded dispute against a proved result.

It reuses the same session family and contract identity as `02.HAPPY.PATH.md`
but exercises the revised portable challenge and repair semantics.

## Deterministic Failure Conditions Anchored By This Artifact

- `CHALLENGE` missing `contractRef`, `contractRevision`, `subjectDigest`, covered digests, or
  canonical digest binding
- `REPAIR` that does not change any covered digest and still attempts to reopen completion
- `REPAIR` treated as resolving the challenge without a later accepted
  `STATE.stateTransitionDigest`
- `STATE phase=completed` emitted while the challenge remains unresolved
- `CLOSE` emitted while the dispute remains active
- challenge depth greater than `maxChallengeDepth`
